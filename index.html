GMU Student Feedback

A comprehensive platform for students, faculty, and administrators to share and analyze feedback for courses, teaching methodologies, and university facilities.

Key Features

Role-Based Access

Different interfaces and capabilities for students, faculty, and administrators to ensure everyone has the right tools for their needs.

Comprehensive Feedback

Collect detailed feedback through various question types including ratings, text responses, and multiple choice options.

Powerful Analytics

Visual reports and detailed analytics help identify trends and areas for improvement in courses and teaching methods.

How It Works

1

Sign Up

Create an account with your GMU credentials and select your role as student, faculty, or administrator.

2

Provide Feedback

Students can submit detailed feedback for their courses, while faculty can review feedback for their classes.

3

Analyze Results

View comprehensive analytics and reports to identify trends and make data-driven improvements.
